Revolut AI Challenge
From transaction data to financial Intelligence
At Revolut, we don't just want to show users where their money went; we want to help them make smarter financial decisions for their future. Transaction categorization remains a major industry challenge:
 Supermarket purchases may be tagged as Shopping instead of Groceries
 Inaccurate categorization leads to poor budgeting insights
 Users struggle to understand their spending habits
Notes
© 2026 Revolut. All rights reserved.

Core Challenge
Design a framework for an AI system that improves Revolut's spending analytics. Your goal is to outline the logic behind the solution, but we also encourage teams to build a proof of concept that demonstrates how key elements of their approach could work in practice.
Your framework must explain the following three components:

Categorization Logic
How would your system automatically assign an accurate category to a transaction? (e.g., based on merchant name, time, amount, user history).

Insight Engine
Once transactions are categorized, what is one specific, valuable insight your system would provide to a user? (e.g., alert them when they are overspending in a category, identify a new recurring subscription).

User Interaction
How would the user receive this insight? (e.g., a push notification, a summary in the app).
